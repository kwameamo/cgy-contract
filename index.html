Introduction

This T-Shirt Design Client Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of , by and between CurioGraphicsYard ("Designer") and ("Client"). This Agreement outlines the terms and conditions governing the t- shirt design services provided by CurioGraphicsYard.       • One original t-shirt design

  • Standard Plan
    • Price: $80 USD (or equivalent in Client’s currency)
    • Includes:
      • One original t-shirt design

  • Premium Plan
    • Price: $250 USD (or equivalent in Client’s currency)
    • Includes:
      • One original t-shirt design
      • Unlimited major and minor revision
      • Mockup presentation of the design on a t-shirt
      • Final design delivered in high-resolution PNG, vector formats, mockup files, and source files
      • Priority turnaround time

5. Revisions and Rejection Policy

  • Revision Allowance:
    • Basic Plan: Includes up to 2 minor revisions.
    • Standard Plan: Includes up to 2 major revisions and 3 minor revisions.
    • Premium Plan: Includes unlimited major and minor revisions.
  • Definitions:
    • Major Revision: Significant changes that alter the core concept or elements of the design.
    • Minor Revision: Includes up to 2 major revisions and 3 minor revisions.
    • Premium Plan: Small adjustments that do not significantly change the overall design, such as colour tweaks or slight modifications.
  • Rejection Clause:
    • If the Client is not satisfied with the initial design concept, they must provide detailed feedback specifying the aspects they wish to change.
    • Revisions will be made in accordance with the plan’s revision allowance.
    • If, after all included revisions have been exhausted, the Client still rejects the design, any additional revisions will incur an extra fee of $10 USD per minor revision and $25 USD per major revision

6. Delivery Timeline

  • Designer will deliver initial design concepts within [X] business days upon receipt of the deposit and all necessary materials from the Client.
  • Revisions will be completed within [Y] business days after receiving feedback from the Client.
  • For Premium Plan clients, priority turnaround times will be discussed and agreed upon separately.

7. Ownership and Rights

  • Upon full payment, the Client will obtain exclusive rights to the final design(s).
  • Designer retains the right to use the designs for portfolio and promotional purposes unless otherwise agreed in writing.

8. Confidentiality

  • Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information disclosed during the project.

9. Termination

  • Either party may terminate this Agreement with written notice.
  • In the event of termination, Designer retains the deposit, and the Client will be invoiced for any work completed beyond the deposit amount.
